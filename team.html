Arsenal's co-founder

Arsenal waxaa aas'aasay David Danskin sanadkii 1886 marka david ayaa af co-founder
waa aas'aasaha kooxda waana nin ka ka danbeeyay maanta inaan aragno kooxdaan xooga badan.

Arsenal's ceo

Arsenal wxaa Milkiile ama ceo ka ah Richard Garlick. wuxuuna xilkaan la wareegay sanadkii 2021
waana nin kooxda wax badan ka badalay xaga dhaqaalaha kooxda iyo dhanka ku guuleysiga premier league-ga
waana ninka hadda si sax ah u leh kooxda Arsenal

Arsenal's coach

Choach ka Arsenal waxaa la dhahaa Mikel Arteta waana macalin wax badan ka badalay kooxda
wuxuu imaaday sanadkii 2020 wuxuuna kooxda ka dhigay mid awood badan leh kana awood badan kooxaha premier league
wuxuu ku guuleysatay koobab badan oo ugu weyn yahay premier league waana macalin tactics badan leh.

Arsenal's captain

Arsenal waxaa captain ka eh Martin odegard waa leader daacad ah dadaal badan hard-worker ah kooxda wax badan kusoo kordhiyay
koobab waa weyn kula soo guuleysatay wuxuuna captain hayaa illa 3 Years wuxuu u dhashay dalka norway
waana captain xooga age kiisa iska tahay dhex-dhexaad
